🌾 About Farm2Home

Farm2Home is an innovative web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers. Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture and provide fresh farm products directly to households. We aim to support local farmers, reduce food wastage, and ensure healthy living for our customers.
